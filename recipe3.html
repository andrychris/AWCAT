Salmon with Creamy Feta Cucumbers.

INGREDIENTS.

1 tbsp. olive oil

2 lemons

4 5 oz. skinless salmon fillets

Kosher salt and pepper

1 lb. seedless cucumbers, sliced on a bias

3 oz. feta cheese

1/4 c. plain Greek yogurt

1/4 c. small mint leaves, roughly chopped
